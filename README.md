4. Project Description
Campus connect is a centralized academic platform that helps 
university students and lecturers manage course materials and 
Q&A in one place. Many students currently use different platforms 
such as chat groups and external websites to find learning 
materials or ask questions, causing confusion as information is 
not organized and important discussions can easily be lost.
The system provides a structured platform where:
- Lecturers can upload and manage course materials
- Students can access resources based on their subjects
- Students can post questions and receive answers from 
  other students or lecturers
- Roles such as Student, Lecturer and Admin ensure proper 
  control and content management
